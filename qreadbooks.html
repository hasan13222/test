Wattpad is a online storytelling site that has grown to be very popular among teenagers and young adults. Ranging from romances to adventures to science fiction, people can publish their novellas online for the world to see

Some of the authors on Wattpad have gained such stardom on the site, they’ve caught the eyes of publishers. People as young as seventeen have been able to secure writing contracts with the likes of publishing houses such as Random House and Simon & Schuster
